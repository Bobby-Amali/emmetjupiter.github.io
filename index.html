Our Purpose

Griot and Grits is a new take on the West African Griot, the storytelling, singing, poet, historian of the village that was tasked with passing on the history of the village orally.

Our mission is to preserve the history of the African American experience one voice at a time using AI and other advanced technologies.

Record

Create a comprehensive, searchable library of recorded oral and video accounts of the lives of local African Americans.

Improve

Use AI to highlight key events from the recorded content and associate them with similar events to create a richer story.

Display

Generate a geographical representation of the stories to allow individuals to search by location.

Collect

Recreate a documentary-like view of individual stories with the assistance of AI to augment key events with additional relevant background information, photos and videos.

Work Of The Project

The Griot and Grits Project will bring the African American experience to life for our generation and generations to come by building innovative technology that does the following:

#empty needs stuff

Enable users to upload video and audio recordings to a centralized repository or library.

Extract metadata and key events from the uploaded content using AI.

Enrich the metadata and key events with information, videos, audio and pictures from the public domain using the assistance of AI.

Annotate the uploaded video and audio recordings with the enriched content using AI.

Use the assistance of Generative AI to further enhance the enriched content with additional contextual information, videos, audio and/or pictures.

Build an index of searchable content based on the metadata and key events.
